# 🌿 Insight: Multilayer Farming Roadmap

Welcome to the **Insight Multilayer Farming** documentation. This roadmap provides a structured path for farmers to transition to a sustainable **Multilayer Model**.

## 🗺️ Project Mindmap

```mermaid
mindmap
  root((Insight: Multilayer Farming))
    Core Philosophy
      Zero-Budget Natural Farming (ZBNF)
      Mimic Natural Forest
      Maximize Vertical Space
      Low Capital / Organic Inputs
    Vertical Layers
      Layer 1: Underground
        Roots: Ginger, Turmeric
      Layer 2: Surface
        Leafy Greens: Spinach, Coriander
      Layer 3: Creeper
        Vines: Gourd, Bitter Gourd
      Layer 4: Canopy
        Trees: Papaya, Mango
      Support
        Bamboo Structure and Netting
    5-Phase Roadmap
      Phase 1: Preparation
        Source Desi Cow
        Stop Chemicals
      Phase 2: First Application
        Jeevamrutha
        Beejamrutha
      Phase 3: Growing Season
        Maintenance
        Pest Spray
      Phase 4: Harvest and Evaluate
        Data Collection
        Comparison
      Phase 5: Scale-Up
        Expand from Test Plot
    Advanced Technology
      Bot: Farm Scheduler
      Alerts: Weather and Irrigation
      Monitoring: IoT Soil Sensors
      Detection: AI Plant Disease
      AI Assistant: Local Knowledge
      Network: Community Farmer App
    Resource Management
      Soil: Jeevamrutha
      Pest: Neemastra, Agniastra
      Water: Drip Irrigation
      Mulching: Straw and Grass
    Assessment
      Soil Health: Earthworms, pH
      Climate: Bangladesh Adaptation
      Economy: Cost Reduction
```

## 🚀 How to Use This Roadmap

1.  **Assess Your Land**: Start with [Land Evaluation](/docs/multilayer-farming/access-land-and-climate-condition) to check if your soil and drainage are ready.
2.  **Learn the Layers**: Understand how to [select and stack crops](/docs/crop-selection-and-layering) for maximum yield.
3.  **Follow the Phases**: Move through the [Execution Phases](/docs/execution-phases) step-by-step, starting with a small test plot.
4.  **Integrate Tech**: Explore [Advanced Technology](/docs/advanced-technology) to automate reminders and monitor soil health.
5.  **Manage Pests Organically**: Use [Pest & Disease Management](/docs/pest-and-disease-management) techniques like Neemastra.

---

_This project is adapted for the Bangladesh context, focusing on local crop varieties and affordable technology._
