# Phase 2: First Application (Week 3)


> **Skill Level:** 🟢 Beginner to 🟡 Intermediate
> **Time:** 7–14 days
> **Goal:** Prepare and apply Jeevamrutha, treat seeds, establish mulch, plant first ZBNF crop

---

## Daily Tasks

### Day 1–2: Prepare Jeevamrutha

- [ ] 🟢 Clean 200L plastic drum (rinse, no soap)
- [ ] 🟢 Add: 200L water + 10 kg fresh cow dung + 5–10L cow urine + 2 kg jaggery + 2 kg pulse flour + 1 handful fertile soil
- [ ] 🟢 Stir clockwise with wooden stick for 2–3 min
- [ ] 🟢 Cover with gunny bag (NOT airtight). Place in shade.
- [ ] 🟢 Stir twice daily (6 AM + 6 PM) for 48–72 hours

**Mistakes to avoid:** Metal drum kills microbes. Airtight lid suffocates them. Direct sun overheats. Use only fresh dung.

### Day 2–3: Fermentation Check

- [ ] 🟢 After 48 hrs: sweet-sour smell + slight bubbling = ready
- [ ] 🟢 In BD heat (30°C+), usually done in 48 hrs. Winter: give 72 hrs.
- [ ] ⚠️ Rotten/putrid smell = failed batch. Discard and restart with looser cover.

### Day 3: Prepare Beejamrutha

- [ ] 🟢 Tie 5 kg cow dung in cloth bag → soak in 20L water for 12 hours
- [ ] 🟢 Dissolve 50g lime in 1L water separately. Let sit overnight.
- [ ] 🟡 Next morning: squeeze cloth bag thoroughly into water
- [ ] 🟢 Add 5L cow urine + lime water + 1 handful fertile soil. Stir well. Ready immediately.

### Day 4: Treat Seeds

- [ ] 🟢 Pour Beejamrutha over seeds in flat tray, coat every seed
- [ ] 🟢 Dry in **shade** (NOT sun) for 2–4 hours
- [ ] 🟡 For seedlings: dip roots 20 min in Beejamrutha before transplanting
- [ ] 🟢 Light tilling only (4–6 inches max, preserve soil structure)

### Day 5–6: Apply & Plant

- [ ] 🟢 Pour Jeevamrutha evenly on test plot soil
- [ ] 🟢 Plant treated seeds or transplant dipped seedlings
- [ ] 🟢 Follow intercropping layout (see `/docs/crop-selection-and-layering/`)
- [ ] 🟢 Light watering to settle soil around seeds

### Day 7–10: Establish Mulch

- [ ] 🟢 Spread rice straw/jute sticks/dry leaves **3–4 inches thick** on all bare soil
- [ ] 🟢 Leave 2-inch gap around each stem (prevents rot)
- [ ] 🟡 Sow dhaincha or cowpea as living mulch between crop rows

### Day 11–14: Observe & Record

- [ ] 🟢 Walk plot daily — check germination, mulch condition, soil moisture
- [ ] 🟢 Start farm log: date, applications, observations
- [ ] 🟢 Set reminder for next Jeevamrutha (15 days from first)

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Rotten smell | Sealed too tight | Restart with loose gunny bag cover |
| Poor germination | Seeds dried in direct sun | Always shade-dry treated seeds |
| Can't find mulch | Season timing | Store rice straw after harvest; use water hyacinth after drying 3-4 days |
