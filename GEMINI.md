# Insight - Multilayer Farming Roadmap

## Project Goal
This project provides a comprehensive, structured roadmap for farmers to transition to **Multilayer Farming**. It combines traditional sustainable farming techniques (like Akash Chaurasia's multilayer model) with modern technology (IoT, AI bots, and data-driven alerts) to maximize productivity on small landholdings, particularly adapted for the Bangladesh context.

## Documentation Structure
The documentation is organized following a 12-step roadmap:

1.  **Assess Land**: Soil, fertility, and drainage evaluation.
2.  **Crop Selection & Layering**: Choosing compatible crops for different vertical layers.
3.  **Execution Phases**: Step-by-step implementation from preparation to scale-up.
4.  **Pest & Disease Management**: Organic and tech-driven pest control.
5.  **Advanced Technology**: IoT monitoring, automation bots, and smart alerts.
6.  **Research & Findings**: Initial experiments and localized data.

## Standards
- **Folder Structure**: Each major topic is a folder with an `_index.md`.
- **Navigation**: Uses `hugo-book` theme. Sections are collapsible.
- **Visuals**: Complex processes and structures are represented using **Mermaid** diagrams.
- **Front Matter**: Every page must have a `title`, `weight`, and `bookFlatSection: true`.
